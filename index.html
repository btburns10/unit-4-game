Crystal Gem Game

You will be given a random number from #19-120 at the start of the game.

There are four crystal gems located below. The value of the gems are hidden until clicked. By click on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The objective of the game is to match your score to the random target number. You lose the game by increasing your score above the target number.

Each time the game restarts, the value of the target number and each gem will change. Good Luck!
